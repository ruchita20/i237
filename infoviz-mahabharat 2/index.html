Decoding the Mahabharata - One Relation at a Time

About 1.8 million words in total, the Mahabharata is roughly ten times the length of the Iliad and the Odyssey combined, or about four times the length of the Ramayana.

Hover over a name to see how this character is related to others in the story.

On a side (and light) note, we found this really great article that talks about the hypothetical scenario of Facebook being there during the Mahabharata. Our visualization provides a way to see the social fabric of characters in the Mahabharata.
